This was a research that investigates the trend of one of the largest sharing economy platform, Airbnb. Originally, the Airbnb manifesto stated that Airbnb fosters social interactions between hosts and guests. However, there is an ongoing debate saying that Airbnb is becoming a purely business transaction platform. Therefore, the aim of this research was to find out the answer for this question. Using Python pandas and numpy, did data anaylsis on Airbnb reviews of each different cities around the world to analyse the trends of Airbnb and expressed these trends by plotting graphs. Cooperated with team members and manage the data and codes using Github. Using the data analysed and graphs ploted, showed that that social interaction factors of Airbnb is decreasing and Airbnb turning into a business-oriented platform.
